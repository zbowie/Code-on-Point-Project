About the Author

Meet Zorrae!

Zorrae recently relocated to the Bay Area from Lawrence,KS to pursue an opportunity through AmeriCorps as a Volunteer In Service to America (VISTA) with a local non-profit, Success Centers. She enjoys roller skating, yoga, crafts, tea, trying new recipes, hiking, and reading! Zorrae is motivaed by feeling grounded, connected, and creative in her work.

With a backround in Psychology and a dedication to helping others she transitioned to this role with the hopes of making a positive and lasting impact on the organizations programs and community. During her time working with Success Centers she functioned as an assistant to the Community Relations Department. She also provided event support, and worked within teams to provide technological and communication support. The largest projects undertaken included service learning projects with students from the University of San Francisco where she worked with students to create a video project about Success Centers. She also worked to define the role, rules, and responisibilities of a volunteer coordinator and developed systems for tracking volunteer progress through Salesforce. Overrall, Zorrae attempts to allow her work to be informed by the needs of the organization on a macro level to fascilitate conversations around sustainable practices and policies.

She is now pursuing her aspirations of working in Real Estate, she is looking forward to making a career change and excited about where it will lead her!

Things she enjoys about the Bay Area include:

  • The diverse landscape
  • The food and events
  • The street arts culture and museums
